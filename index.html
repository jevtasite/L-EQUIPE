ALL UNITED - A COLLECTIVE OF AGENTS, ONE COMMON VISION

At L'ÉQUIPE, everything starts with our core values: humility, honesty, and respect. These principles unite and guide every action we take. Because we believe that unity makes strength, we have created an agency that reflects these convictions—solid, humane, and engaged.

We combine Swiss precision with over 25 years of experience in professional football. Our expertise goes far beyond the pitch, offering administrative and financial support to simplify your procedures and secure your choices, psychological and educational guidance for constant progress, and a human approach that recognizes your career as a personal adventure.

We are present by your side in victories and defeats. More than simple support, we offer a true alliance of trust to move forward together toward your goals. Every career is a strategic campaign, and we approach each player's journey with tactical precision, ensuring optimal positioning at every stage of professional life.
